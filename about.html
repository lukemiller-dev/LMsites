About Me

I started building websites in high school and there haven't been many days where I haven't been on my computer since. I grew up in the Northern Virginia and I am currently based in Fairfax, VA. I am a huge Washington Redskins and Capitals fan, which may get me in trouble with potential clients from Philly and New York, but when it comes to building you a website or redesigning an application, we can leave sports out if it until we finish the project.

In today's market, if you run a business, 90% of the time you want to have some part of it online. Whether it's just a brochure site or 100% online, having a website for your business is almost mandatory these days. There are many online tools you can use to do it yourself, but like most things, you get what you pay for. If you have the time and the capability, using a site like Wordpress or Squarespace may be a good option. You could also go to a web design business, but they will likely over charge you to fill their payroll.

So why would you want me to work for you? I'm right in the middle. I provide the same professional web design/development and Search Engine Optimization for half of what most web design companies charge. I truly enjoy helping people get their businesses and ideas online. Whether you just need a potential customer to find your phone number on a google search or you want to create a platform to share your voice to an online audience, I can help you. Send me an email or give me a call so we can start to talk about your project. I'm looking forward to hearing from you.
